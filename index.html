Nenasa Educational App

Nenasa is a FREE educational app available on all mainstream operating systems, which acts as a mobile frontend to the National LCMS e-Thaksalawa as well as an aggregator of educational and edutainment content from a variety or sources.

Nenasa 1916

The Nenasa 1916 service is operated by the State Ministry of Education Reforms, Open Universities and Distance Learning Promotion in collaboration with Dialog Axiata to provide O/L and A/L students with a helpline to connect with teachers to get their queries resolved by dialling 1916 from any telephone network at no cost.
